# How to Submit – TechAscend Cohort 2 (Week 1)

Follow these instructions carefully. Incorrect submissions may not be reviewed.

---

## Step 1 – Create Your Repository

Create a NEW public GitHub repository using this naming format:

### Full Stack
techascend-week1-fullstack-FirstnameLastname

### AI
techascend-week1-ai-FirstnameLastname

Example:
techascend-week1-fullstack-AmaraNdi

Your repository must be PUBLIC.

---

## Step 2 – Required Files

### Full Stack Track
Your repository must contain:
- index.html
- style.css
- README.md

### AI Track
Your repository must contain:
- week1.py OR week1.ipynb
- README.md

---

## Step 3 – Required README Format

Your README.md must include:

# TechAscend Cohort 2 – Week 1

Name:
Track:
Slack Username:

## What I Completed
-

## Challenges Faced
-

## What I Learned
-

---

## Step 4 – Git Requirements

You must make at least 3 commits.

Example commit messages:
- "Initial setup"
- "Added structure"
- "Completed assignment"

Single-commit repositories will not receive full marks.

---

## Step 5 – Slack Submission Format

Post in your track channel using this exact format:

WEEK 1 SUBMISSION

Name:
GitHub Repo Link:
Number of Commits:

Example:

WEEK 1 SUBMISSION

Name: Amara Ndi
GitHub Repo: https://github.com/amara/techascend-week1-fullstack-AmaraNdi
Commits: 4

---

## Deadline

Sunday – 11:59 PM

Late submissions may be marked incomplete.

---

## Important

- Do not send screenshots.
- Do not DM submissions.
- Do not use Google Drive.

All submissions must follow the structure above.
