# Workshop Structure – TechAscend Cohort 2

Workshops occur every alternate Saturday (unless replaced by a major event).

They are structured, skill-accelerating sessions.

---

## Purpose

Workshops are designed to:

- Deepen technical skills
- Strengthen practical understanding
- Improve professional readiness
- Address common blockers

---

## Possible Topics

- Git deep dive
- Debugging strategies
- Clean project structure
- AI workflow fundamentals
- Resume & portfolio building
- Interview preparation
- Communication skills

---

## Workshop Format

Duration: 90–120 minutes

Recommended Structure:

1. Introduction (10 minutes)
   - Topic overview
   - Why it matters

2. Deep Dive (40–60 minutes)
   - Demonstration
   - Live walkthrough
   - Example implementation

3. Practical Exercise (20–30 minutes)
   - Guided hands-on activity

4. Q&A (20 minutes)

---

## Expectations

- Attendance required
- Active participation encouraged
- Cameras on (if virtual)
- Preparedness expected

Workshops are acceleration sessions.
Take them seriously.
