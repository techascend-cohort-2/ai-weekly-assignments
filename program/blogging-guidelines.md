# Blogging Guidelines – TechAscend Cohort 2

Every alternate Saturday is Mandatory Blogging Day.

This is not optional.

---

## Purpose

Blogging helps you:

- Strengthen communication skills
- Document technical growth
- Build professional visibility
- Improve clarity of thought
- Create public proof of learning

Engineers who communicate clearly stand out.

---

## Where to Publish

You may publish on:

- LinkedIn
- Medium
- Dev.to
- GitHub (as a structured README entry)

---

## Required Structure

Title:

"What I Learned in Week X – TechAscend Cohort 2"

Your post must include:

1. What you built
2. What challenged you
3. What you learned
4. One example (code snippet or explanation)
5. What you will improve next week

Minimum length:
400–600 words.

---

## Submission

Post your blog link in Slack using:

BLOG SUBMISSION – WEEK X

Name:
Link:
Track:

Deadline:
Saturday 11:59 PM

---

## Evaluation Criteria

- Clarity
- Honesty
- Technical explanation
- Professional tone

Blogging is part of your professional training.
